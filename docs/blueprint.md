# **App Name**: Destiny Tour & Travels

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Display of travel agency information, location in Kangra, Himachal Pradesh and featured destinations with high-quality images.
- Services Page: Detailed information about taxi services, Innova, Tempo Traveller, Alto, and Sumo, including booking options and pricing.
- Contact Page: Contact information with a contact form, agency address, phone number, and WhatsApp link for easy communication.
- About Page: Information about the travel agency's mission, vision, and history to build trust with customers.
- Call and WhatsApp Buttons: Prominent call and WhatsApp buttons for quick customer inquiries and bookings.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy orange (#D2691E), reminiscent of the vibrant sunsets over the Himachal mountains.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC) for a warm and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Muted green (#8FBC8F), reflecting the lush landscapes of Kangra.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a modern, humanist sans-serif suitable for comfortable reading.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif adding elegance.
- Use clean, modern icons representing services and destinations.
- Clean, grid-based layout for a modern and tourist-friendly design.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
